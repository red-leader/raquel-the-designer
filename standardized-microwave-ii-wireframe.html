Standardized Microwave II: Wireframe

Part II of HCI II Final Project.

Requirements: Take feedback from napkin test and design a new product mocked-up as a wireframe. Test design on 3 users.
UX Test results: Biggest complaint from users was overabundance of features. Users were divided about value of additional functions. 
Design implications: Selection process should be streamlined and guide the users more about how to input their desired function. Consider value of maintaining additional features; ensure users will have confidence in them if kept.
